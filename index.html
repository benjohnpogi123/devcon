About DWCC IT

The School of Information Technology at Divine World College Of Calapan is dedicated to providing high-quality education in the field of Information Technology. Our programs are designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed to thrive in the rapidly evolving IT industry.

Our experienced faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and industry-relevant curriculum ensure that students receive a comprehensive education that prepares them for successful careers in IT. Whether you are interested in software development, cybersecurity, data science, or any other IT specialization, DWCC IT is the place to turn your passion into expertise.

Sport Complex of DWCC

Our state-of-the-art sports complex provides students with excellent facilities to engage in a variety of sports and physical activities. From basketball courts to a well-equipped gymnasium, we encourage students to maintain a healthy and active lifestyle.
